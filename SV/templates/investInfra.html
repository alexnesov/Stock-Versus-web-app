Options

Options are a fantastic tool. Not only because they allow leverage. Leverage alone can ruin you.
They can actually allow the not-so-rich guy to spread the risk more easely whil having the opportunity to actually make some more interesting profit.
Indeed, spreading the risk on 10 000 dollars while trading casual stock won't bring so much.
With options the risk will be similar, but the potential upside way higher.

Recall this:

Trading the stock:
Buy 100 shares of stock at $50/share = $5,000 investment.
Sell 100 shares of stock at $54/share = $5,400 return (8% ROI)


Trading the option:
Buy 1 $50 100-share option contract at $1.00/share = $100 investment
Sell 1 $50 100-share option contract now worth $2.00/share = $200
This is a 200% ROI
